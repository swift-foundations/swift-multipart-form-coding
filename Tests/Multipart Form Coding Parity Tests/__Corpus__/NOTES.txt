  and Form.Data.Entry.List.multipartContentType(boundary:) (:228) both accept an
  injected boundary; generateFormBoundary() only fires when boundary is nil.

  was needed (B2 Boundary.random() item: no forced-random API path found).

- Bool.Encoder strategies (.trueFalse/.yesNo/.numeric, dossier B2-06) are part of
  swift-url-routing's RFC_2046.Multipart.Encoder surface and do NOT exist in this
  package or its dependency stack (grep over swift-rfc-2046/-7578/swift-whatwg-html
  sources found no such strategies). No array strategy axis exists on this surface
  either: Form.Data.Entry.List represents repetition as repeated entry names.
